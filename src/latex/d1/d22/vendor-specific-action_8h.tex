\begin{DoxyCompactList}\small\item\em the organization identifier is a public organizationally unique identifier assigned by the I\+E\+EE.

Similar to protocol field of data packets that indicates which entity of higher layer should handle received packets, Organization Identifier field is used in Vendor Specific Action frames to indicate which entity or higher layer should handle vendor specific content. Normally the value is assigned by I\+E\+EE and the length of field is either 24 bits or 36 bits.
