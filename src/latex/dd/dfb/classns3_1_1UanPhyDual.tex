\subsection{Detailed Description}
Two channel Phy.

A class that wraps two generic U\+AN Phy layers (\hyperlink{classns3_1_1UanPhyGen}{Uan\+Phy\+Gen}) into a single P\+HY. This is used to simulate two receivers (and transmitters) that use the same front end hardware. When attached to a \hyperlink{classns3_1_1UanTransducerHd}{Uan\+Transducer\+Hd}, this results in a net device able to transmit on one or two channels simultaneously or receive on one or two channels simultaneously but that cannot transmit and receive simultaneously.

Many of the standard P\+HY functions here become ambiguous. In most cases information for \char`\"{}\+Phy1\char`\"{} are returned.
