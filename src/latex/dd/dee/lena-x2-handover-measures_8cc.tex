Sample simulation script for an automatic X2-\/based handover based on the R\+S\+RQ measures. It instantiates two e\+NodeB, attaches one UE to the \textquotesingle{}source\textquotesingle{} e\+NB. The UE moves between both e\+N\+Bs, it reports measures to the serving e\+NB and the \textquotesingle{}source\textquotesingle{} (serving) e\+NB triggers the handover of the UE towards the \textquotesingle{}target\textquotesingle{} e\+NB when it considers it is a better e\+NB.
