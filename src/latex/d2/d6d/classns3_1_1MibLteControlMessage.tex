\subsection{Detailed Description}
Abstract model for broadcasting the Master Information Block (M\+IB) within the control channel (B\+C\+CH). 

M\+IB is transmitted by e\+NodeB R\+RC and received by UE R\+RC at every radio frame, i.\+e., every 10 milliseconds.
