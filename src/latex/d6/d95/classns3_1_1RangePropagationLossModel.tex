\subsection{Detailed Description}
The propagation loss depends only on the distance (range) between transmitter and receiver. 

The single Max\+Range attribute (units of meters) determines path loss. Receivers at or within Max\+Range meters receive the transmission at the transmit power level. Receivers beyond Max\+Range receive at power -\/1000 d\+Bm (effectively zero).
