\subsection{Detailed Description}
The {\ttfamily ns-\/3} attribute system is the mechanism used in {\ttfamily ns-\/3} to organize, document, and modify the {\itshape values} used by the various component models.

Attributes also enable the tracing and statistics gathering in the simulator.

See \hyperlink{group__attributehelper}{Attribute Helper} for macros to ease the declaration and definition of Attributes.
