\subsection{Detailed Description}

\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em channel\+Number} & channel number that the S\+CH service can be made available for communications. \\
\hline
{\em operational\+Rate\+Set} & Operational\+Rate\+Set if present, as specified in I\+E\+EE Std 802.\+11. valid range\+: 1-\/127. \\
\hline
{\em immediate\+Access} & Indicates that the M\+L\+ME should provide immediate access to the S\+CH and not wait until the next S\+CH interval. \\
\hline
{\em extended\+Access} & Indicates that the M\+L\+ME should provide continuous access (during both S\+CH interval and C\+CH interval) to the S\+CH for Extended\+Access control channel intervals. A value of 255 indicates indefinite access. \\
\hline
{\em edca\+Parameters} & If present, as specified in I\+E\+EE Std 802.\+11. \\
