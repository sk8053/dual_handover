\subsection{Detailed Description}
Defines the T\+CP option of kind 4 (selective acknowledgment permitted option) as in \{2018\}. 

T\+CP Sack-\/\+Permitted Option is 2-\/byte in length and sent in a S\+YN segment by a T\+CP host that can recognize and process S\+A\+CK option during the lifetime of a connection.
