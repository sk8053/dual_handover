\subsection{Detailed Description}
Abstract model for broadcasting the System Information Block Type 1 (S\+I\+B1) within the control channel (B\+C\+CH). 

S\+I\+B1 is transmitted by e\+NodeB R\+RC and received by UE R\+RC at the 6th subframe of every odd-\/numbered radio frame, i.\+e., every 20 milliseconds.
