\subsection{Detailed Description}
Service Access Point (S\+AP) offered by the A\+NR instance to the e\+NodeB R\+RC instance. 

This is the {\itshape A\+NR S\+AP Provider}, i.\+e., the part of the S\+AP that contains the A\+NR (Automatic Neighbour Relation) methods called by the e\+NodeB R\+RC instance.
