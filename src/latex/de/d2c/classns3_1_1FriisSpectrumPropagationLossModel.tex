\subsection{Detailed Description}
Friis spectrum propagation loss model. 

The propagation loss is calculated according to a simplified version of Friis\textquotesingle{} formula in which antenna gains are unitary\+:

$ L = \frac{4 \pi * d * f}{C^2}$

where C = 3e8 m/s is the light speed in the vacuum. The intended use is to calculate Prx = Ptx $\ast$ G
