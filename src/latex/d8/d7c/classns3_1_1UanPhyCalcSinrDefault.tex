\subsection{Detailed Description}
Default S\+I\+NR calculator for \hyperlink{classns3_1_1UanPhyGen}{Uan\+Phy\+Gen}.

The default ignores mode data and assumes that all rxpower transmitted is captured by the receiver, and that all signal power associated with interfering packets affects S\+I\+NR identically to additional ambient noise.
