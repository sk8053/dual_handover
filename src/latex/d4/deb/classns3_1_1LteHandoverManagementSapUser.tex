\subsection{Detailed Description}
Service Access Point (S\+AP) offered by the e\+NodeB R\+RC instance to the handover algorithm instance. 

This is the {\itshape Handover Management S\+AP User}, i.\+e., the part of the S\+AP that contains the e\+NodeB R\+RC methods called by the handover algorithm instance.
